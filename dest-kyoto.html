Kyoto

Welcome to Kyoto — Japan’s timeless heart.
From the tranquil gardens of Gion to the golden glow of Kinkaku-ji, Kyoto blends centuries of tradition with a quiet, modern charm. Wander through ancient temples, taste matcha in a teahouse, and watch the seasons paint the city
in colors of cherry blossom and autumn maple. Kyoto is where Japan’s soul lives on.
Every street tells a story — from the sound of bamboo in Arashiyama to the soft shuffle of a kimono on Hanamikoji Street. Discover calligraphy, tea ceremonies, and the artistry of simplicity that defines Kyoto’s beauty.
