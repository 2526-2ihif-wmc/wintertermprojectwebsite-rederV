Thailand

About the Country

Thailand is a country of vibrant culture, stunning landscapes, and rich history. Explore Bangkok, the bustling capital full of grand temples and lively markets; Chiang Mai, a northern city surrounded by mountains, ancient temples, and rich traditions; and Phuket, a tropical paradise of beaches, islands, and crystal-clear waters.

Best Places to go to

India is a land of incredible diversity and culture. Explore Delhi, the bustling capital full of historic landmarks and vibrant markets; Jaipur, the “Pink City” with stunning palaces and forts; and Kerala, a tropical paradise of backwaters, beaches, and lush landscapes.

Delhi

Delhi

India’s capital blends history and modernity, from the Red Fort and Qutub Minar to lively streets, bustling markets, and rich cuisine.

Jaipur

Jaipur

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for its magnificent palaces, forts, colorful bazaars, and royal heritage.

Kerala

Kerala

Kerala offers serene backwaters, palm-lined beaches, and lush hills, providing a peaceful and scenic escape in southern India.

Food & cuisine

Indian cuisine is a celebration of flavor, color, and tradition. Each region has its own specialties, from spicy curries and tandoori dishes to sweet treats and street food. Meals often combine fragrant spices, fresh ingredients, and a balance of textures, reflecting India’s rich cultural heritage and regional diversity.
