Featured Festivals

Chinese New Year

February 17–22

Celebrate the Lunar New Year across China, especially in Beijing, Shanghai, and Hong Kong. Streets come alive with colorful parades, traditional lion dances, fireworks, and family gatherings, creating an atmosphere full of joy and tradition.

Holi

March 3

Join the Festival of Colors in India, particularly in Mathura, Vrindavan, and Delhi. The streets explode with vibrant powders, music, and dancing as people come together to celebrate the arrival of spring.

Lantern Festival

March 24–25

Experience the Lantern Festival in cities like Pingxi in Taiwan or many towns across China. Beautiful glowing lanterns fill the night sky, marking the end of Chinese New Year with hope, light, and celebration.

Songkran

April 13–15

Celebrate the Thai New Year in Bangkok, Chiang Mai, and Phuket. Streets turn into playful water battlegrounds as locals and tourists splash, dance, and take part in traditional temple blessings.

Hanabi Taikai (Fireworks Festivals)

July–August

Watch summer fireworks light up the skies in Japan, with the largest displays in Tokyo, Osaka, and Nagaoka. Families and friends gather along rivers and parks to enjoy dazzling pyrotechnic shows.

Eagle Hunting Festival

October 5–6

In western Mongolia, especially around Bayan-Ölgii, watch hunters demonstrate ancient falconry traditions. Riders showcase the skill and bond between humans and majestic golden eagles on the open plains.

Esala Perahera

July 22–August 1

Attend the grand procession in Kandy, Sri Lanka. Dancers, drummers, and elaborately decorated elephants parade through the streets honoring the Sacred Tooth Relic, creating a magical and spiritual atmosphere.

Diwali

November 8

Celebrate the Festival of Lights across India, especially in Jaipur, Varanasi, and Mumbai. Homes, streets, and temples glow with lamps and fireworks, as families share sweets and prayers for prosperity.

Cheung Chau Bun Festival

June 4-5

Experience the Cheung Chau Bun Festival on Hong Kong’s Cheung Chau Island. Colorful parades, towering bun displays, and lively performances fill the streets as locals and visitors celebrate with music, dances, and traditional rituals.

Illumination Festivals

December

Experience Japan’s winter Illumination Festivals in Tokyo, Osaka, and Kobe. Streets, parks, and buildings glow with dazzling lights and tunnels of color, creating magical evenings perfect for strolling, photos, and festive cheer.
