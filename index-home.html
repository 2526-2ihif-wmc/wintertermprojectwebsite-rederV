Upcoming Festivals

Harbin Ice and Snow Festival

January

Experience the Harbin Ice and Snow Festival in China’s Heilongjiang province. Massive ice castles, intricate sculptures, and colorful lights transform the city into a stunning winter wonderland that draws visitors from all over the world.

Chinese New Year

February 17–22

Celebrate the Lunar New Year across China, especially in Beijing, Shanghai, and Hong Kong. Streets come alive with colorful parades, traditional lion dances, fireworks, and family gatherings, creating an atmosphere full of joy and tradition.

Holi

March 3

Join the Festival of Colors in India, particularly in Mathura, Vrindavan, and Delhi. The streets explode with vibrant powders, music, and dancing as people come together to celebrate the arrival of spring.
